Serve-a-thon Fundraiser

Sponsor student service to make Christian education affordable to all and serve our community at the same time!

It’s just like a race-for-education, but instead of sponsoring a runner your sponsee serves others like the Elizabethtown Borough, Unto (formerly Gain), a food pantry, and Cornerstone Youth Ministries.

Any donation or EITC pledge (scroll down for more EITC info) received before the May 7th service date will count towards student fundraising incentive milestones, like silly stringing a teacher or getting a pizza party. The students’ ultimate goal is $25,000.

Tax deductible donations can be mailed to: The Kelly School, PO Box 69, Elizabethtown, PA 17022

EITC: Fund scholarships instead of paying PA taxes

It’s true! Businesses and individuals who donate scholarships to The Kelly School can receive a 90% PA tax credit through the EITC program.

See more here or contact Mary Dodds, director of operations, at info@thekellyschool.com, (717) 489-2590.

Volunteer, Field Trips, & Job Shadows

Elementary students do a field trip every month. Middle and High School students have field trips plus school choice week each semester when students choose what they study from service dogs to job shadowing a judge.

Have a field trip opportunity, a workshop to teach, or a job to shadow? Shoot us a line at info@thekellyschool.com.

In-Kind Gifts

Want to donate office or school supplies, furniture or electronics? Please see our wish list or send us an email at info@thekellyschool.com to let us know what you have.
